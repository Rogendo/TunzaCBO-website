The Ultimate Dream

I am a founder member of a community-based organization called Tunza Community Based Organization. The CBO was registered in 2017 under the Ministry of Labour, Social Security, and Services. The CBO was registered in order to help tackle the many challenges faced by kids who come from a humble background in various public primary schools in rural parts of Bomet County. These challenges include lack of school uniforms, school shoes, writing materials and sanitary pads to the girls. These challenges expose these kids to various dangers like contracting diseases from pit latrines which are mostly not in good condition. The girls also miss schools during their monthly periods because they do not have the pads this, in the end, leads to poor performance in school. These kids are also affected mentally in that they look down upon themselves because they do not have good uniforms and shoes.

Short Story

Tunza Community Based Organization is a non-profit organization run by Mr.Kevin Lang'at.

Here in Tunza we are motivated to help and care for the needy pupils in Bomet. We do so by providing the Basic neccesities a growing kid needs in order to make the learning more comfortable.

What we offer to the society

We issue the basics needs for example;

  • sanitary towels,
  • school shoes,
  • school uniforms,
  • school bags and also writing materials.

    • We have also developed a mentorship program in order to make sure that these kids are taken care of in terms of their spiritual, mental and social lives.
    This venture is important because we are providing solutions to challenges faced by kids in public schools in Kenya. This, in turn, will bring about a positive impact in the lives of these kids and even the lives of their families.
